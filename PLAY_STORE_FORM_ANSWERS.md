## Store Listing

- Short description: `Play Android music on HomePod and AirPlay-compatible speakers.`

Full description:

```text
Android2Apple4Music is a free Android app for playing allowed Android audio on HomePod, Apple TV, and AirPlay-compatible speakers on the same Wi-Fi network.

Use it for music apps that allow Android playback capture:
1. Connect your Android phone and Apple speaker to the same Wi-Fi network.
2. Open Android2Apple4Music.
3. Scan for your HomePod, Apple TV, or AirPlay-compatible receiver.
4. Tap Connect and approve Android's audio capture prompt.
5. Play Spotify or another allowed music app.

The app can mute the phone speaker while streaming and can sync the HomePod volume with the phone media volume.

Android2Apple4Music does not bypass Apple FairPlay, DRM, subscriptions, or app capture restrictions. Some apps may block playback capture for protected content.

Made free by PelPush.com Accounting, MarkCompass.com, RealArb.com, and MarcPelberg.com.
```

## Permissions Explanation

- Record audio: captures allowed Android playback audio only after the Android system prompt is approved.
- Media projection foreground service: keeps the user-approved audio capture session active while streaming.
- Internet/network: discovers and streams to receivers on the local Wi-Fi network.
- Wake lock: keeps playback alive while connected.
- Notifications: shows active streaming status and a Stop action.
- Modify audio settings: supports muting local phone playback and syncing media volume.
