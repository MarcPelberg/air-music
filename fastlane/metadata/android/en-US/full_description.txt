Android2Apple4Music is a free Android app for playing allowed Android audio on HomePod, Apple TV, and AirPlay-compatible speakers on the same Wi-Fi network.

Connect your Pixel or Android phone to a nearby AirPlay-compatible receiver, approve Android's capture prompt, then play music from Spotify or another app that allows Android playback capture.

Features:
- AirPlay-compatible local network playback
- HomePod discovery with manual IP fallback
- Phone speaker mute guard while the speaker plays
- Phone volume sync and in-app speaker volume control
- Visible foreground service with a Stop action
- No ads, no analytics, and no developer-operated cloud audio storage

Android2Apple4Music does not bypass Apple FairPlay, app DRM, subscriptions, or capture restrictions. Some apps may block playback capture for protected content.
